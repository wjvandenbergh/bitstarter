We all have that one game, the one with the multitude of tiny little pieces. The one where one lost piece ruins the gaming experience. Or the one that you can't take on the road because the jostle of travel plays havoc with your carefully organised components. You've tried plastic baggies. You've tried elastic bands and little boxes and foam inserts. None of these do the job. In the end, pieces get lost, components get damaged, and your beloved game gets relegated to the shelf or replaced.

Enter Box Commander. Custom made component containers fitted into an easy to transport container. Boardgame pieces are kept safely in place in labelled storage slots, with component counts on hand so you never lose a piece again!

FAQ

Why Box Commander?
Because it's something we wish we had. We love our boardgames, but we hate unpacking, repacking, recounting and eventually losing components. We hate carrying boxes around to gaming days and scuffing or damaging them. It's time for something better.
